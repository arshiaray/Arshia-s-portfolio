About Me

I'm an aspiring web designer and developer. I've recently graduated from university and take a deep interest in all things tech and design. Growing up, the internet as we know it was a fairly novel thing and I was immediately fascinated by it. I wanted to know what makes websites and games work and look the way they do which pushed me to pursue Computing in GCSE. During that time, I learnt a lot about back-end development and I loved it, it tickled my brain in the right way and I knew I wanted to revisit this again sometime in the future. So here I am, 5 years later delving into the world of tech through front-end development and loving every bit of it. Outside of tech, I love cooking for my loved ones, consuming music and art and having a go at making them myself, and spending time in nature.
